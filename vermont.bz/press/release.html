Release of vermont.university


January 14, 2017

As result of negotiations with various organizations and partners:


We at Pollen Agency are excited to present https://vermont.university on behalf of the now forming trust: Vermont Estate Ltd.


This Top Level Domain, or TLD, is the product of newly established entities and sits at the highest level of the Domain Name System, or DNS, hierarchy.


On behalf of Calvin and Jordan Lafland: We at Pollen present entirely, wholly, to the Trustees of the University, ownership of https://vermont.university. Zero strings.


The gift of this property is also made on behalf, in accordance with the will, of the Alumni Council of the University of Vermont. I wish the upmost best for and thank both the University and the Alumni Council, which supports it, for their tremendous work spanning 225 years.



Respectfully,
